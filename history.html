History of ITB


    • Institute of Technology Blanchardstown was established in 1999 and is a third level institution. ITB provides part-time and full-time courses in higher education in a wide range of courses. In January 2019 ITB will join the Institute of Technology Tallaght (ITT) and Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) to form ‘Technological University of Dublin” (TUD).It will be Irelands eight University but Irelands first technological University.
