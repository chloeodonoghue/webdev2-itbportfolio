Introduction to ITB

Institute of Technology Blanchardstown was established in 1999 and is a third level institution. ITB provides part-time and full-time courses in higher education in a wide range of courses. In January 2019 ITB will join the Institute of Technology Tallaght (ITT) and Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) to form ‘Technological University of Dublin” (TUD).

The Institude of Technology Blancharstown has a number of diffrent courses to offer students ranging from Computer Studies, Business, Sports and Horticulture studies. The institude is very accsible as it is walking distance from Blancharstown centre. It also runs along the bus routes of the 239, 38, 38A and the 270. The college also runs its own shuttle bus leaving every hour dropping students off at Blancharstown centre and Coolmine Train Station.
