The future of TUD

The wait for the opening of TUD has seemed like a long time coming. The wait for permission to open the Technological Univeristy of Dublin took nearly 7 years but hopefully it will be worth the wait. TUD will become Irelands first technological univertisy in January 2019. As the opening of TUD becomes close I think of a lot students and staff are wondering what will change about the campuses now that ITB, ITT and DIT will become one big university. I decided to interview a student from each college to get their opinion on what they think should change now that these three institudes are becoming a university.
